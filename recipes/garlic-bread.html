Garlic Bread

Description

Craving something toasty and garlicy? This garlic bread recipe will surely satisfy your cravings.

This recipe ensures you get your garlic bread hot and ready in less than 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons softned butter
  • 1 crushed clove garlic
  • 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
  • Ground black pepper
  • 1/4 sliced large baguette
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill to high heat.
  2. Mix together butter, garlic, Parmesan cheese, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Spread the butter mixture generously on the baguette slices.
  4. Place slices on the preheated grill and cook until butter is melted and bread is lightly brown, about 30 seconds.
  5. Sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately.
