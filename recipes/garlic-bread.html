Garlic Bread

Description

Craving something toasty and garlicy? This garlic bread recipe will surely satisfy your cravings.

This recipe ensures you get your garlic bread hot and ready in less than 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons softned butter
  • 1 crushed clove garlic
  • 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
  • Ground black pepper
  • 1/4 sliced large baguette
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
