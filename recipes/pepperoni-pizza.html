Pepperoni pizza

Description

If you ever wanted a pepperoni pizza, but not the carbs to go with it, then this is the perfect recipe for you. This recipe will make your pizza crisp, crunchy, and oh yeah, LOW CARB. This is Greg's recipe on ruled.me and here's how to make it:

Ingredients

Pizza Base

  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 tablespoon psyllium husk powder
  • 3 tablespoons cream cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
